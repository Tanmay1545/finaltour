Victoria Memorial

Location:

Queens Way,Maidan,Kolkata West.

Description:

Victoria Memorial in Kolkata is an architectural masterpiece, dedicated to Queen Victoria, showcasing a blend of Indo-Saracenic and British styles, surrounded by lush gardens, and housing a museum with a rich collection of art and artifacts from India's colonial past.

Howrah Bridge

Location:

Kolkata,West Bengal

Description:

Howrah Bridge in Kolkata is an iconic cantilever bridge, spanning the Hooghly River and connecting Howrah to Kolkata. Renowned for its engineering brilliance, it stands as a symbol of the city's historic and cultural significance.

AC Cab: Rs.28 (Cost per km.)
Bus: Rs.2800 (Mumbai-Kolkata)
Metro: Rs.35 ( 2.3 kms Maiden Metro Station)
