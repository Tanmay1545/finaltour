Palm Resort, Bangalore

Excellent 4.5 / 5

If a tranquil getaway, without the stress and hassles of city life is what you’re looking for, Palm Meadows Resort is here to assist you in doing just that. The sprawling Resort offers the ultimate relaxation with surprises in every part of the property that are nothing short of utmost luxury. The Palm Meadows Resort is spread over 5 acres of lush green with a Corporate Getaway and state of the art facilities located in close proximity to the IT corridor Whitefield and 55 minutes away from the International Airport. Recognized as a pioneer amongst luxury resorts, it is the first choice for corporate and leisure travellers of Silicon Valley of India. Palm Meadows Resort offers the most indulgent experience with a variety of dining options, activity area for kids, an outdoor swimming pool, a superlative spa and a state-of-the-art fitness centre, making it one of the best resorts in Bangalore.
